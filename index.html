I am a researcher with main focus on low power design techniques. I am intersetd in low power architectures, cache architecture, pipeline and design aspects of low power devices for IoT applications and embedded systems. To read more about my research please click here.

Demands for low power operation

The increasing demand for battery operated devices in the scope of mobile computing and Internet of Things (IoT) applications has created a shift in the design paradigm of modern electronic devices. There are various academic and industrial efforts to enable ultra-low power operation for IoT device. The underlying questions to be addressed includes:

  • How feasible these efforts are
  • What are the price to pay and their gains as well.
  • More importantly how are these solutions accepted in the industries and high-tech fabrications.
